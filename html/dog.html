Dog Training Tips

Dogs: the adorable domestic pet beloved by millions. The dog is one of the most popular pets in the world -- it is, of course, commonly referred to as 'man’s best friend'. Dogs are also often employed as service animals and emotional support animals.

The best dog in the world for one person could be a nightmare for someone else. When searching for a new dog, it is important to avoid breeders that are more interested in making money than producing quality dogs for appropriate homes. Breeding dogs for profit is controversial, because there are already many more dogs than available homes.

Simple Dog Training Tips

Getting your Dog to Sit and Stay

Step:1 Stand in front of your dog and show the dog a treat.

Step:2 Move treat to dog, say sit as dog sits,reward with treat.

Step:3 Praise your dog behavior. Rub his head and use words such as "good boy".
